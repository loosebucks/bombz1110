Simpl3r: Bounty Hunters Deep Dive 11/4/2022

by elMihi

Simpl3r: Bounty Hunters is a project with a 8k supply that came out in mid September and had a mint price of 2 SOL (SOL was 33$).

What Is Simpl3r: Bounty Hunters
Simpl3r: Bounty Hunters is a utility based project.
The main driver behind the project is the utility.

Utility
The utility can be separated into 2 categories: Simpl3r and Protection.
- Simpl3r
Simpl3r is a community management platform designed for Web3 teams and helps projects with analyzing crucial data related to: sales, marketing, WL, socials and community.
Some examples of the utility:
Sales Hub - provides industry-first insights into customer buying habits.
Marketing Hub - helps you easily manage your marketing and create viral community engagement.
Engagement Hub - allows teams to get detailed information about the levels of engagement they are getting across platforms such as Discord and Twitter.
Commit Now, Pay Later - allows teams to enable and incentivize collectors to commit to a mint ahead of time.
NOTE - there are more sub-utilities in all of the listed sections, I have only stated to most basic explanation (more info in WP).
- Protection
V3rify - an advanced holder verification system designed to deliver seamless onboarding of community members.
Prot3ct - a protocol that automatically deactivates any NFT in your collection that has not been bought with royalties included, by either removing your IP and swapping the image with a warning or by placing an overlay over the original image warning the purchaser to pay the full royalties or the NFT will be deactivated.

Overview
There is a lot more info regarding staking, NFT customization and a WL marketplace but in the interest of keeping this as short as possible if you want more info go check out their WP (its a long read but worth it).
The amount of utility and building this projects contains makes me excited to see what else they can bring to the table.
Giving out early BSL vibes for sure.

Meta Tattoo Club Deep Dive 10/31?2022

by elMihi

Meta Tattoo Club is a project with a 8888 supply that came out in late October and had a mint price of 2 SOL
(SOL was 28$).

What Is Meta Tattoo Club
Meta Tattoo Club is an IRL based project.
The main driver behind the project is the IRL utility.

Utility
The native token is called $INK (no LP atm) and can be earned by staking.
Staking has two forms: Shop staking and Drink the Ink.
Shop staking is a normal version of vanilla staking while Drink the Ink is a gamefied version where you can lvl up your MTC and earn more $INK.
$INK can/will be used in a couple of ways like:
- IRL tattoos - use $INK to pay for IRL tattoos
- Virtual tattoos - use $INK to get your avatar tattooed, first tattoo shop in the Sandbox
- Ink vial - use $INK to purchase an Ink Vial, lvl up your Ink Vial to lvl 5 in order to upgrade your NFT and earn more $INK daily while staking.
- Merch and giveaways

Overview
One of the team members & globally recognized tattoo artist Andreas Ishak is the owner of Saints Touch tattoo studio in Gothenburg, Sweden.
Every month one lucky holder will be getting flown out to Sweden & will receive an 8hr tattoo session, airplane flights, hotel accommodation, spending cash & will be able to bring their +1.
The team started construction of their second shop in Canggu, Bali and is looking to expand into California and Western Canada.
